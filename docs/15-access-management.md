# Access Management

## Principles
- Least privilege by default.
- Just-in-time access for privileged roles.
- MFA required for administrative access.

## Controls
- Privileged Identity Management (PIM) for elevated roles.
- Quarterly role reviews for critical subscriptions.
- Break-glass accounts monitored and restricted.

## Evidence
- PIM activation logs
- Role review reports
- Access recertification records
