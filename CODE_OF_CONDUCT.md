# Code of Conduct

This repository follows a simple, professional code of conduct to keep collaboration respectful and productive.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Focus on the work and assume good intent.
- Give constructive feedback and accept it gracefully.
- Keep discussions professional and on-topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxing or sharing private information.
- Intimidation or sustained disruptive behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security advisory or contact the repository maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including removing content or restricting participation.
